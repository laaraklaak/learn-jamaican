Riid Wa Yu Si (Read What You See)

These activities will test your reading comprehension and your ability to recognize correctly spelled Jamaican words. Remember to pay close attention to the long vowels (like aa and ii) and the diphthongs (ai, ie, ou, uo).

Rait Wa Yu Ier (Write What You Hear)

These activities will test your ability to write Jamaican words correctly. Remember the golden rule: "write what you hear." Pay attention to special consonant rules, like using j for the 'dr' sound and k instead of 'c' or 'ck'.

Need a refresher? Review Lesson 2: The Rules of Writing →

Listen & Write: Long Vowels

Test your listening skills! Hear the Jamaican words spoken and type the correct spelling. A great way to practice the "rait wa yu ier" rule.
